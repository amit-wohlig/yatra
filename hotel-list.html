Featuring an outdoor pool and a swimming pool, Trident Nariman Point is located in Mumbai and offers lively accommodation. It provides 5-star accommodation and views of the sea.

There are a variety of facilities on offer to guests of The Trident Nariman Point, including an express check-in and check-out feature, an executive floor and 24-hour room service. It offers babysitting services, a currency exchange and a reception that operates 24 hours a day.

Rooms at Trident Nariman Point are spacious and feature a mini bar and movies-on-demand. Each provide bottled water, a seating area and a private bathroom with a shower.

A satisfying breakfast is prepared every morning at Trident Hotel Nariman Point, and there are numerous cafs and eateries close by.
